FitBitBot:

Project Healthbot

Our project aimed to address overall mental health and wellbeing as reflected by the quantity and quality of sleep. Project Health bot is a Facebook messenger chatbot that was supposed to be conncected to the Fitbit system. After a week of data, the chatbot would have recieved sleep data from Fitbit through a program in python.
